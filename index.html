About Me

I'm a passionate DJ based in Nairobi, dedicated to bringing the fresh blend of music, vibe, culture, and energy to every event. My mission is to create unforgettable OUTSIDER experiences by curating sets that celebrate the richness and energy, breaking away from the traditional music experience into how music should be experienced while keeping the crowd engaged and the vibe alive.

Whether it's a club night, private event, or festival performance, I blend traditional African rhythms with contemporary beats to create a unique sonic experience that resonates with diverse audiences. Every set is crafted with intention, bringing authenticity and energy to the dance floor.
